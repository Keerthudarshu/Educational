Eligibility Criteria

NEET Coaching

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in PCB
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Clear entrance test (if applicable)

JEE Coaching

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in PCM
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Clear entrance test (if applicable)

CET Coaching

  • Completed 10+2 from recognized state board
  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics subjects
  • Minimum 45% aggregate
  • Domicile of the state required

Foundation Course

  • Currently studying in Class 8th, 9th, or 10th
  • Minimum 60% in previous class
  • Interest in Science and Mathematics
  • Regular attendance commitment
