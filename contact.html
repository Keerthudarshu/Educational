Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for admission?

You'll need mark sheets of 10th and 12th (or latest class), ID proof, passport size photos, and any previous coaching certificates if applicable.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, comprehensive study materials are included in the course fee. This includes textbooks, practice papers, and online resources.

Is there any entrance test for admission?

For some courses, we conduct an aptitude test to assess the student's current level and provide appropriate batch allocation.

Do you offer online classes?

We offer hybrid learning with both offline and online options. Online classes are available for students who cannot attend physical classes.

What is the refund policy?

Refunds are processed as per our policy. Please contact our office for detailed information about refund terms and conditions.

Do you provide scholarship programs?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and financial assistance programs for deserving students. Contact our admission desk for details.
